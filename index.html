Track curated music lists

Discover, listen, and keep your progress


Music is deeply personal.
Groove Ledger exists to help you explore intentionally.

🎵 Track

Keep track of albums you've listened to across curated lists like Rolling Stone’s 500 Greatest Albums and Apple Music rankings. Mark items as listened and gradually build your personal journey through music history.

Instead of relying on memory or scattered notes, Groove Ledger gives you a clear and structured way to follow your progress over time — one album at a time.

📊 Progress

See your progress across each list and stay motivated as you go. Understand what you've completed and what’s still waiting to be discovered.

Whether you're just starting or deep into a list, Groove Ledger helps you visualize your journey and keep a steady pace without feeling overwhelmed.

📚 Ownership

Own your listening history. Your progress is yours — a personal record of your exploration through music.

No algorithms, no social pressure, no performance metrics. Just a private and meaningful reflection of what you've experienced and what matters to you.

🎧 CURATED

Thoughtful curation over infinite scrolling. Focus on meaningful album lists instead of endless recommendations.

Lists are not absolute truths — they are guides. Groove Ledger helps you navigate them intentionally, at your own pace, with your own perspective through an overwhelming catalog.
